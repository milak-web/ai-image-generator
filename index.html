DEEP FIX GUIDE

The 504 and 413 errors are caused by proxy limits. To fix them forever:

  1. Edit your webui-user.bat file.
  2. Replace your command line args with this:
set COMMANDLINE_ARGS=--api --cors-allow-origins *

3. Restart Stable Diffusion and turn on Direct Mode in this app.
